Case study: SaaS Scheduling Solution

A B2B SaaS startup wanted to validate if dental clinics were experiencing issues with phone scheduling, their core problem their product solved.

Using ReviewLens, they analyzed 10k+ dental clinics' reviews and found:

  • 43 clinics had multiple reviews mentioning phone booking problems
  • 22 of those had reviews within the last 6 months
  • 7 converted to paying customers within 60 days

Result: 16% conversion rate on targeted outreach, 3x better than previous cold outreach.
